Amsterdam is one of the largest small city and capital of the Netherlands. From Amsterdam canals to world-famous Amsterdam museums and historical Amsterdam sights, it is one of the most romantic and beautiful cities in Europe. Canal cruises are a popular way to see the city from the perspective of its canals. The small scale of the buildings and the intimacy of the streets, canals and squares create an atmosphere that visitors find unique.
