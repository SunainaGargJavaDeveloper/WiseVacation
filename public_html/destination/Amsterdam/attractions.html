With its original houses, canals and parked bikes everywhere, the city centre looks like a modern day painting. Speaking about paintings, the Rijksmuseum is a must visit. You have to see the ‘Night watch’ at least once. One of Amsterdam’s other highlights is the touching museum of Anne Frank. Don’t forget to order tickets online since the queue is way too long.
