Berlin Regional Airport-Bml

Berlin Regional Airport is a public airport located seven miles (11 km) north of the central business district of Berlin, a city in Coos County, New Hampshire, USA. This general aviation airport covers 575 acres (233 ha) and has one runway. It once had scheduled air service via Air Vermont and at one point Northeast Airlines..
